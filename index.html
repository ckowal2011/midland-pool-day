How it decides

👶 Toddler Mode

  • Stricter on UV, heat, AQI, PM10, wind, and dust.
  • Penalizes midday UV more heavily from 11 AM to 4 PM.
  • More likely to recommend morning/evening windows or indoor play.

🧑 Adult Mode

  • More tolerant of heat, UV, wind, and moderate air quality.
  • Still rejects dangerous heat, poor AQI, high dust, and high rain/storm chances.

Location note

  • Midland: dust risk considers dry/windy West Texas conditions.
  • Sewickley: dust risk is usually lower, but AQI/PM and storms still matter.
